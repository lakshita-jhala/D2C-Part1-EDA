# Business Memo

## Key Findings

- Customers with low recency scores show higher churn risk.
- High-frequency customers demonstrate stronger engagement and retention.
- Customers with low monetary contribution are more likely to become inactive.
- Champions and Loyal Customers generate the highest revenue.
- At-Risk customers represent an important retention opportunity.

## Recommendations

1. Launch targeted retention campaigns for At-Risk customers.
2. Reward Champions and Loyal Customers through loyalty programs.
3. Encourage repeat purchases among low-frequency customers.
4. Use RFM segments for personalized marketing communication.
5. Monitor customer engagement regularly to identify churn signals early.

## Conclusion

RFM analysis successfully identifies valuable customer segments and highlights groups requiring retention efforts. Future churn prediction models should incorporate RFM-based features.
